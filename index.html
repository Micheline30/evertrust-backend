Mindset Before You Handle Any Objection
You are the DOCTOR — they are the PATIENT. They filled out a request and raised their hand. You are not selling anything. Their "objections" are simply asking for clarification on how to reach their goal — protection for their family. Stay in control. Answer every question with a question. Whoever asks the most questions wins.
🔑 KEY WORDS & POWER PHRASES — Use These Every Time
Magic Words
"Perfect" — Meet them where they are, then guide back
"Listen" — Commands attention: "what I'm saying matters"
Urgency Phrases
"I don't have a lot of time"
"I can put you down between appointments"
"I'll have 15 appointments tomorrow"
"I know how important this is to you"
✅ The Close Question (Yes/Yes): Always end with "What time is better — [time A] or [time B]?" — Never ask yes/no. Always give two choices, both of which move forward.
